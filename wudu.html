How to Perform Wudu (Ablution)

Step-by-step guide to make Wudu correctly — followed by a clear video tutorial you can watch anytime.

Quick steps
  1. Make the intention (niyyah) to perform wudu.
  2. Say “Bismillah” (In the name of Allah).
  3. Wash both hands up to the wrists three times.
  4. Rinse the mouth three times.
  5. Inhale water gently into the nose and blow out three times.
  6. Wash the face thoroughly three times.
  7. Wash the right forearm up to the elbow three times, then the left.
  8. Wipe the head once with wet hands (masah).
  9. Wipe the inner and outer ears once.
  10. Wash the right foot up to the ankle three times, then the left.

These are general steps in accordance with the Sunnah — local madhhab details may vary slightly.
